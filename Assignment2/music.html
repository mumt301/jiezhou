Plini's music

I'm here sharing some of his songs from the album impulse voices. Plini produced, composed, and organised this album. Simon Grove was the co-producer, engineer, and mixer. Due to COVID, Chris Allison on drums and Simon Grove on bass were able to contribute to the album as additional members. Devesh Dayal of the Indian/American progressive metal band Skyharbor and Aleksandra Djelmash also worked on the album's production, and they were joined by guests Dave Mackay on piano and synthesiser, John Waugh on saxophone, and Amy Turk on electro-acoustic harp.
